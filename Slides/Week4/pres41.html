Introduction
Finding high frequency terms and their word correlations are useful for
exploratory analysis when you want to get a general idea of what your
corpus is about.
But as researchers, we often want to count a list of specic words.
This is where text analysis dictionaries come in.

Text Analysis Dictionaries
In text analysis circles, a dictionary is simply a list of domain-specic
words or phrases.
These dictionaries are then applied to the documents in the corpus,
perhaps to see how frequently the words/phrases in that list occur.
These dictionaries are domain-specic in the sense that they are
usually carefully designed by area experts to uncover latent qualities
of document producers, as expressed in their written language.
